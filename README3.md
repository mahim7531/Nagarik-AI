# NagarikAI – Bangladesh Smart Civic Intelligence Platform

## 🚀 Overview

NagarikAI is an AI-powered civic engagement platform designed to connect citizens directly with government authorities and local administrations in Bangladesh.

The platform enables citizens to report road damage, missing persons, public safety hazards, environmental issues, and city infrastructure problems through a smart, user-friendly interface. Using Artificial Intelligence, real-time mapping, and government dashboards, NagarikAI helps authorities identify, prioritize, and resolve civic issues faster and more efficiently.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Citizens often struggle to report public issues effectively. Reports are lost, delayed, or ignored due to lack of transparency and communication between citizens and government agencies.

NagarikAI solves this by providing:

* Direct issue reporting
* AI-powered issue analysis
* Real-time status tracking
* Government response monitoring
* Public transparency and accountability
